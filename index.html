Privacy Policy

AI Summary Research Extension — Last updated: June 28, 2026

This extension is operated by researchers at the University of Washington for academic research purposes.

What data we collect

When you participate in this study, the extension collects:

  • Your Prolific participant ID (self reported)
  • Your preferred search engine and account sign-in status
  • The AI-generated summary text shown to you on either Google, Bing, and Yahoo Search for each study query
  • Citation links and source URLs included in those summaries
  • The URL of the search results page and your browser's user agent string
  • Location information, including IP-address
  • Timestamps of each capture

What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, or any browsing history outside of the specific study queries.

How data is stored

Data is transmitted over HTTPS to a secure server and stored in a password-protected database accessible only to the research team. Data is retained for the duration of the research project and handled in accordance with University of Washington IRB guidelines.

How data is used

Data is used solely for academic research analyzing how AI search summaries. Findings may be published in aggregate form; no individual participant will be identifiable in any publication.
